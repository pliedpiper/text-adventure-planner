================================================================================
                              SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

BACKGROUND IMAGES:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Format: PNG (for transparency support) or JPG
- Style: Dark, atmospheric, slightly desaturated
- Lighting: Predominantly low-light, nighttime scenes

MUSIC FILES:
- Format: WAV, OGG, or MP3
- Suggested length: 2-4 minutes, loopable
- Style: Dark ambient, minimal, unsettling
- Tempo: Slow to moderate

SOUND EFFECTS:
- Format: WAV or OGG
- Length: 1-10 seconds typically
- Quality: 44.1kHz, 16-bit minimum

================================================================================
                              STYLE NOTES
================================================================================

VISUAL STYLE:
- Color palette: Deep blues, blacks, desaturated warm tones (for home)
- Contrast: High contrast, deep shadows
- Mood: Isolated, intimate, increasingly otherworldly
- References:
  * "What Remains of Edith Finch" (home interiors)
  * "Anatomy" by Kitty Horrorshow (dark house horror)
  * "Layers of Fear" (psychological horror atmosphere)

AUDIO STYLE:
- Influences:
  * Akira Yamaoka (Silent Hill series)
  * Ben Frost (dark ambient)
  * Lustmord (deep drone ambient)
- Key elements:
  * Low drones
  * Subtle dissonance
  * Found sounds
  * Organic textures for underground sections
