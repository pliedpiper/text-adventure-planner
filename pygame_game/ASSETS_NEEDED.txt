================================================================================
                              SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

BACKGROUNDS:
  - Resolution: 1280x720 (game window size)
  - Format: PNG (transparency) or JPG
  - Style: Dark, atmospheric, desaturated
  - Lighting: Low-light, nighttime

MUSIC:
  - Format: WAV, MP3, or OGG
  - Length: 2-4 minutes, loopable
  - Style: Dark ambient, minimal
  - Volume: Normalized, not too loud

================================================================================
                              QUICK START
================================================================================

Don't have assets yet? The game runs fine with placeholders!

For quick testing, you can:
1. Use AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E) for backgrounds
2. Use royalty-free dark ambient music from:
   - freesound.org
   - incompetech.com
   - Free Music Archive

Prompt for AI backgrounds:
"Dark atmospheric [scene description], horror game background,
nighttime, cinematic lighting, slightly desaturated, 16:9 aspect ratio"
